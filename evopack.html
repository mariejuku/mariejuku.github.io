Pack Description

A pack of official Evo signs in various sizes and colours. Anyone can use these freely. The pack includes five colour schemes. After downloading, you may safely delete the colour folders you don't plan to use.

Installation

Copy the 'skins' folder from the archive and paste it into your Trackmania 2020 user folder. When updating the pack, overwrite any previous files.
