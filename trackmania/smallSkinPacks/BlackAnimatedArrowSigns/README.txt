Black Animated Arrows Signs for Trackmania (2020)
Author: eFFecT

For each size (1x1, 2x1 and 4x1), the bottom, left, right and top variants exist.
It also includes a white background for each size.
The main purpose is to place them on top of the Nadeo Valley, Snow, Desert background signs.
They increase the visibility compared to the white Nadeo ones.

Where to put the signs? Extract here, including the locator files!
[Your_Documents_Folder]\Trackmania\Skins\Any

For example: C:\Documents\Trackmania\Skins\Any

Credits to Nadeo for the original white animated signs, these ones are based on them.
